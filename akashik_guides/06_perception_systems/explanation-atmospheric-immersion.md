# User Guide: Atmospheric Immersion (NomadPR)

**Version:** 3.2.6
**Role:** Narrative Depth and Background Ambience

---

## 📻 The Nomad Radio Protocol
The **NomadPR** system provides a collection of 17+ high-fidelity radio broadcasts designed to deepen immersion during gameplay. These files sound like authentic Night City broadcasts, covering everything from real estate rumblings to local neighborhood greetings.

### 📻 Available Broadcasts:
- **`Agents of Desire`**: Deep dive into the underworld's elite.
- **`A Night At The Opera`**: High-society corporate intrigue.
- **`Real Estate Rumble`**: Economic shifts in the Glen and Little Europe.
- **`Welcome to the Neighborhood`**: Onboarding-themed local flavor.
- ... and 13 others.

---

## 🛠️ How to Trigger
Atmospheric audio can be triggered via the **Dashboard Mesh** or programmatically by the **Director** based on narrative triggers.

### ◈ Manual Trigger (Dashboard)
Navigate to the `AUDIO` tab in the Nucleus Deck and select a broadcast from the `NomadPR` library.

### ◈ AI-Driven Triggers
The Director (Node B) is trained to trigger specific broadcasts when players enter a new district or complete a mission objective. For example, entering a Combat Zone might trigger `Bathed In Red`.
