## ⚡ THE NEURAL HIVE: LINGUISTIC FOLDING
Linguistic Sovereignty ensures that the "Time of the Red" is not a monolith. Every faction and district in the mesh operates through procedurally generated dialects (conlangs), creating a layered, high-friction communication environment.

## 1. SKILLSTONES: THE MATERIALIZED GRAMMAR
A **Skillstone** is the materialized logic of a conlang. It is a compact specification defining phonology, grammar, and lexicon.

- **DETERMINISTIC_GEN**: Skillstones are derived from numeric seeds for bit-identical consistency.
- **FACTION_BINDING**: Every entity in **Akashik.db** is tethered to a `conlang_seed`.
- **CONTEXT_INJECTION**: The **RED_DIRECTOR** injects these Skillstones into the reasoning context, forcing NPCs to communicate through their native dialect.

### ◈ LINGUISTIC COMMANDS
- `/conlang <seed>`: Preview the materialized Skillstone logic.
- `/conlang <seed> <payload>`: Test linguistic steganography encoding against a specific seed.

---

## 2. SIMULATION INTERACTION
NPCs utilize their conlangs for tactical coordination and information protection.

### ◈ DECRYPTION PROTOCOLS
Operators with **Cryptography** or **Linguistics** parameters can trigger decryption within the Foundry VTT interface.
- **SUCCESS**: Standard translation of conlang to system-primary language.
- **CRITICAL_SUCCESS**: Exposure of hidden steganographic payloads.

---

## 3. LINGUISTIC STEGANOGRAPHY
The simulation uses **Glossopetrae** patterns to hide coordination data within conlang text.

### ◈ COORDINATION CHANNELS
Hidden payloads reveal tactical intents (e.g., "Flank Target", "Request Backup") that are invisible to the unaugmented observer. Detection requires high-level Netrunning or Cryptography checks.

---

## 4. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE
- **NODE_A (RUST)**: Responsible for the high-performance permutation and stego-encoding of linguistic shards.
- **NODE_B (TS)**: Manages the Skillstone Registry and LLM orchestration loop.
- **RESIDENT_SYNAPSE**: The **Open-Reasoner-1.5B** and conlang dictionaries are resident on Node A to ensure zero-latency materialization.
