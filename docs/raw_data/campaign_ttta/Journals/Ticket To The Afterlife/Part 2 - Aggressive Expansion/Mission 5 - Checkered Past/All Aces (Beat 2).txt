For this beat, move Sammy and the players to the casino pit, around one of the poker tables. Any number of players may participate in the game.

Night City Holdem

Table Difficulty: Simple - 3 rounds.

Ante: 2 Eagles.

Sammy heads the table, but doesn't say much - this is, rather, a chance for you to introduce your players to the rules. Gambling rules can be found in the Afterlife Services journal entry #4.

If multiple players pass all 3 rounds, they continue playing until there is a single victor, who takes double the ante. All others who made it past 3 rounds break even. this may not make mathematical sense - if so, oops! Oh well.

Once players have finished their game and are ready to go find Checkers, advance to the next beat.
