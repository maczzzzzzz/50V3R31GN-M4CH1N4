Though information about her sudden seizure is scattered around in the beats throughout this mission, here's the SparkNotes version:

Clarisa got some bad Blue Glass.

a. Her dance instructor, Aaron Kelly/Antonio, is planning a wild XBD (eXplicit/illegal Brain Dance) with women who come to his studio.

b. To make them susceptible to the insanity he has in mind, he's hypnotizing/brainwashing them, KGB style

c. To make them susceptible to activation, they need to be on his version of Blue Glass.

The braindance getup helps make it happen, through poorly-understood means.

The other part of the activation is PARTY ALL NIGHT, which was mistakenly released early/played in the Afterlife at the time of Clarisa's seizure.
