World currencies are unreliable, and the Afterlife knows it.

Instead of relying on the fluctuating value of the Eurodollar or, worse, the overinflated standard of the US dollar, the Afterlife's parent organization mints and distributes small, unique gold coins which only see their full value realized when used in association with the hotel's various services.

These coins are provided as rewards at the end of missions, and can also be distributed as bonuses for inspired play. Each coin is stamped with a picture of Libertas (reimagined in America as Columbia - in both cases, a godess of political liberty) on one side, and an eagle on the other.

These coins can be used to purchase the Afterlife's various services, which are unlocked throughout the game, at a considerable discount compared to what they'd be worth in EB. They can also be sold for 100eb each.
