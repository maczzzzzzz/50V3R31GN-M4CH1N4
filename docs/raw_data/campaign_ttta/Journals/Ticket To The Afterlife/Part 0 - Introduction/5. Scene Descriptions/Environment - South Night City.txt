South Night City - "the bad part of town." The area hit the worst by CW4, and the last area that hasn't been rebuilt. Dominated by the homeless, gangs, and people too desperate to be able to live anywhere else. In short - an edgerunner's paradise.

The streets are pockmarked with craters like a Lace addict's face, and at the district's edges, are being washed into the water. The sturdier buildings are riddled with holes and covered in gang tagging - the less sturdy ones are all in some form of collapse. Rusted out vehicles line the streets, couriers scurry around on skateboards and bicycles, homeless junkies crowd the alleyways, gangs patrol the streets, and the bloody red sunsets make the muddy streets run with blood.

The gunfire that can be heard across half of Night City is closer, here.
