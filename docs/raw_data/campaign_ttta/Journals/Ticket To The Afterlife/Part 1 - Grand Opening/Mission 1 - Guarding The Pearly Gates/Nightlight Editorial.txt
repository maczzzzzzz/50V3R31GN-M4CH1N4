(Nightlight is a recurring character who will be important later, and adds context for the player's actions - read out her editorial at the start of the session, or post it in your group chat!)

The FM Receiver Crackles, The Pirate Wavelength Carrying A Cool, Seductive Female Voice…

"Welcome back my dear Night Owls. This is Nightlight, the voice of the city, here to take the scream off the sheet and into your ears for all our Night Citizens without enough time to read.

"How are we feeling tonight? Tired as you wind down from the day? Groggy as you wake up for the night? Either way Night Citizens, let's hope you get where you need to go. Time for some news.

"Scavenger presence in NC has been on a steep incline, with claims from night citizens that they 'keep multiplying day by day'. Either the friendly neighborhood chop-shop makes enough scratch to call our dear Night Citizens to their cause, people are gettin' desperate in the 15th straight year of depression, or something's pulling them in from out in the badlands; maybe they just couldn't take the taste of sand in their mouths any longer. What do you think?

"In lighter news: I got a message for our edgerunning community. I have it on good authority that something big is on the way - something so big, not even Nightlight can spoil it. All I can say is that it looks like some of you solos will be making it to heaven after all, in the next few months. Lucky you.

"Lastly, the NCPD wants to remind all of you that those cadavers in the gutter are not toys. Please stop giving children human remains. If you spot a body on the street, or anyone providing a child with that spare arm they had lying around, please contact the NCPD. If you see something, say something.

"Batty Karlson's up next, talking about 'whether or not the great bozo was an alien this whole time'. Sounds fun.

"Until later Night Citizens, this is Nightlight, hoping I made your night a little brighter."
