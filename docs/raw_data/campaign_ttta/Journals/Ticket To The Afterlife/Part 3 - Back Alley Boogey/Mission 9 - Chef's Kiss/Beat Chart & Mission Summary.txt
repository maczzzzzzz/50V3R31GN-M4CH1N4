Mission Setup Time: 20-30 Minutes. Most of that time will be spent populating the Gala scene correctly as outlined in Beat 3, as well as learning about the 3 key players that are keeping the event running.

The Afterlife's restaurant needs a kitchen, and the Owner knows just the person. The problem? Their contract isn't exactly up for bid.

Chef's Kiss



Friend Opportunity: Chef William

Chef William has a long and storied history of the eccentric and unorthodox. They adore those who share their passion for all things unusual, while turning their nose up at the mundane. They would be happy to cook for such an individual, every so often.

Enemy Opportunity - Scav Leader/Gala Host/Head of Security

There are several notable figures involved in keeping Fenison captive, including a scav leader, the host of the event where he's being held, and the head of security for the event, all of which have ties to Night Corp.

Rep Opportunity: The Appetizer

Defeat one or more of the event leadership with damage or effects from any type of drug or poison. The player who does so gains the rep bonus.

Gig Payout

350eb + 3 Eagles apiece + a high-quality outfit, with a bonus of 150eb + 1 Eagle if Fenison approves a bonus.
