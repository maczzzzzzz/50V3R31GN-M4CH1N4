Welcome To Hell

Slowly, the world returns. Maybe - the grimy, black-and-white tiles under your feet aren't familiar. Neither is the uncomfortable, lumpy padding of the chair you find yourself in… or the restraints on your arms and legs.

Before you're even fully conscious, there's a loud, monotone ring filling your surroundings, and a moment later, a maniacal cackle sounds over some shoddy-sounding loudspeakers.

"WAKE UP!" The voice, male, shrieks loud enough to peak the mic. Your eyes fly open and you wish they hadn't. Next to you, your fellow edgerunners are similarly restrained in a long string of chairs. The walls are blinking black and white squares, flashing fast enough to be painful to look at.

"WELCOME TO HELL!" the demented voice continues screaming, then laughs insanely again. "OTHERWISE KNOWN AS HIGH SCHOOL! CLASS IS IN SESSION AND YOU'RE. ALL. TARDY! GO! GO GO GO GO GO GO GO!"

You can still hear the laughing as you're suddenly weightless, the floor under you opening and plunging you straight down into blackness.
