This beat takes place in the Clown Dungeon scene, but it varies considerably depending on if the party passed out in beat 4 or not.

If the entire party passed out:

Each character is placed in a chair, in the elevator-adjacent room of the Bozo Lair.

Proceed with Beat 5a.

If at least half the party passed out:

Place the tokens for the player characters in the elevator, indicating those who are prone/have fallen asleep.

Place 1*partycount @UUID[Compendium.night-city-gang-and-corp-mook-pack.NCGangsnCorpsMooks.Actor.6DkEWEDkSFcSGvgS]{Bozo Fools} at the elevator entrance when the door opens.

Proceed with Beat 5b.

If less than half the party passed out:

Place the tokens for the player characters in the elevator, indicating those who are prone/have fallen asleep.

Place 1*partycount @UUID[Compendium.night-city-gang-and-corp-mook-pack.NCGangsnCorpsMooks.Actor.6DkEWEDkSFcSGvgS]{Bozo Fools} around the edges of the elevator-adjacent room.

Proceed with Beat 5c.
