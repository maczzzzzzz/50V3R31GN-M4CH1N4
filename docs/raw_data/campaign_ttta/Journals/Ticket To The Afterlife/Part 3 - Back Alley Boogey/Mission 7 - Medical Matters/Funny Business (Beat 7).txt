This beat takes place in the 7th Circle, which should be much as the party left it with a couple of exceptions:

Most of the Bozos have cleared out, except for a couple which are strung out or hung over around the bar.

There's a dead Bozo on the dance floor, courtesy of Silvers. Most are ignoring it, but some Afterlife staffers arrive to drag it off around the same time the party shows back up.

Vido is still in his booth, and waits for the party with payment in hand.

If nothing else, the 7th Circle is quieter, now. When you return, most of the Bozos are gone, except for a few that are strung out over the bar's tables. A clown corpse, male, is dead on the dance floor, with the Afterlife staff already on the scene to clean up the mess. The corpse is missing its arm and its head, which have already been collected.

Silvers is cleaning one of her swords as she lets you in, leaving little guesswork in what happened to him.

"Bonjour mis vouchers!" Vido calls from the back booth. "How was your time in clown town, eh? Don't mind the body - my understanding is that he got a little handsy, and then lost his head. Now, for the matter of your pay!"
