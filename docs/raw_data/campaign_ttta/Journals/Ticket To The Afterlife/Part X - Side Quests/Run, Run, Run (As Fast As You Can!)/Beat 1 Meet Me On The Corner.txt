Trouble In Heaven

Once the Afterlife's doors slide open, the PCs feel a cool, dry, air-conditioned relief from the city's heat and humidity. Inside, though, an unusual scene greets them.

Gone is their usual chipper concierge, for the first time since they've started working for the club. In Sprinter's place behind the concierge's desk is a haggard looking Sammy, and a not-too-small crowd of disconcerted guests who are swarming him like Piranhas around a keg of Smash.

The PCs overhear the guests expressing their dissatisfaction with the most recent service while Sammy is obviously not the best temp to help out in a customer service role. Most reasonable people would think he was right to snap back at them in disbelief, but the Ice Queen of the Afterlife, Rogue, thinks otherwise. With her cold and distant voice, she reminds him about the Afterlife's goal to achieve unsurpassed customer satisfaction and to take the guests' concerns more seriously.

It's at this point that Sammy spots the party. "Hey!" he shouts, and separates himself from his cloud of problems. The guests look on uncertainly as he all but runs across the lobby towards them.

He asks them if they are busy that night since he's got a fire going and needs them to put it out in exchange for preem pay due to short notice.

Explaining The Situation

Sammy's quick to explain that Sprinter has gone missing.

The circumstances of her flight are unknown - all anyone knows is thanks to the security cameras, which showed her running into the lobby, and a few minutes later, sprinting back out of it with a hastily-packed holdall.

Sammy's worried about the behavior.

In all the time he has known her, Sprinter has been nothing but dedicated to the organisation they mutually work for, and has never before failed to report on-shift.

And while impulsive, Sprinter's always been noted as anything but thoughtless - whatever unexpected event triggered the sudden flight, it must have been huge.

Both Sammy and Rogue are sure it's serious.

The security footage can be provided; a DV17 Human Perception check reveals that Sprinter is in a panic and at least a little frightened.

Sammy thinks it might have something to do with her past.

Sprinter's not ever said much about where she came from, but Sammy knows she has some kind of family back home.

Once the conversation is concluded, Sammy is all but dragged away by irate customers of the Afterlife, and he directs the team towards Rogue for more information.
