Still at the Afterlife

When the PCs head in Rogue's direction, she notices them immediately. In the meantime, she has already taken a seat in her lounge and beckons them over. The surrounding staff immediately clear the way.

Seats and drinks are provided. Rogue remarks that Sammy has probably already informed them of the situation. She would like to have her concierge back before the place goes completely down the drain.

Unfortunately, the Afterlife is currently in some disarray, so none of the regular staff can investigate Sprinter's disappearance.

In addition to the footage from the security cameras, Rogue places a microchip in the centre of the table with additional information about the Afterlife's concierge. The situation is serious and this is reflected in the already cool expression on the Queen of the Afterlife's face.

When the PCs bring up the subject of payment, Rogue immediately brushes it off. First things first. This matter is urgend and any minute wasted is one too many.

After receiving the information, Rogue urges swift action from the PCs and makes herself apologise. Important business. It's a familiar story.
