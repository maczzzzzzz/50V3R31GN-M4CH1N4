The Afterlife is a fully functional establishment! All that's left are some loose ends that the owner wants shored up - certain things that are being required of her. First in line are a cleanup crew, for keeping things… discrete.

Skeletons In The Closet



Friend Opportunity: Bones Malone

There are many strange characters in Night City, but none stranger than the gruesome, skeletally-styled Mr. Malone. He's attracted to - and tends to attract - other extreme individuals, and occasionally, his friend is allowed to miscount their dice results…

Enemy Opportunity - NCPD Sergeant

The NCPD is no stranger to corruption, but the sergeant that's "supervising" Bones and his boys is the worst of the worst. Can be suitably replaced by any enemies with ties to the NCPD or its corruption.

Rep Opportunity: Rattle Em, Boys!

Take a risk to help Bones leave the NCPD a message they'll never forget.

Gig Payout

500eb + 3 Eagles per player, with a bonus of 150eb + 2 Eagles if the party finds what created the info blackout about the operation.
