There's one last thing Rogue and her backers need to really get the Afterlife spinning - and she's been broadcasting across Night City every night from 11 to 1.

This Is Nightlight, Signing Off



Friend Opportunity: Nightlight

Nightlight is a true-blue edgerunner in her own way, and a genuine smooth operator. She'll rub elbows with anyone, but the only people she calls friends are those same folks as her, who utterly ooze edgerunning spirit. And she isn't afraid to boost their rep, either.

Enemy Opportunity - Militech Or Maelstrom Leader

Two of Night City's biggest groups of badasses are trading blows in the ruins of the Old City Center - the commander of either would make for a suitable player enemy.

Rep Opportunity: This Is Nightlight, Broadcasting Live

Get Nightlight on the air while you're rescuing her. If this achievement is passed, every party member present to benefit from the coverage gains a personal rep.

Gig Payout

5 Eagles + 300eb/player upon successful recovery of Nightlight, a rep boost for finishing the campaign, and a couple days of free Afterlife services in celebration of the final services being unlocked.

Beat Chart

Beat 1 - Rogue Signals

Rogue's away on business; Vido rears his head to offer the players their final job in the Afterlife's startup, and to congratulate them on flying so high.

Beat 2 - Dead Air

Nightlight's broadcast clues as to her location lead to the ruins of the Old City Center, an irradiated mess of concrete, steel, Maelstrom and Militech. Secure the site.

Beat 3 - Pirate Wavelength

Having located Nightlight's HQ, the team is confronted with a strange mystery that won't be around for long.

Beat 4 - Guerilla Radio

Time to get Nightlight out, but be careful, you're not the only ones interested in doing so.

Beat 5 - Final Festivities

Back at the Afterlife, a party is being thrown at least in part in the team's honor.
