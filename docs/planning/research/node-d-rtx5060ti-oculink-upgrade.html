3. ik_llama.cpp CUDA Compatibility

  • Blackwell GPUs use sm_100/sm_120 compute capability
  • Requires CUDA Toolkit 12.8+ for native Blackwell support
  • llama.cpp can fall back to PTX JIT compilation
  • NixOS: need bleeding-edge nixpkgs for CUDA 12.8+ and matching NVIDIA driver

5. NixOS Compatibility

OCuLink is transparent to OS. Blackwell requires driver 570.xx series or newer. CUDA 12.8+ is required for native compilation. Both may require nixos-unstable.

6. Power and Thermal

180W TGP. OCuLink enclosure needs separate PSU (400W sufficient). Meteor Lake host platform power is unaffected.
