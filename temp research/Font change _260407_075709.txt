**Retro Cyberpunk Fonts (ready for your theme-sync.ts)**

Here are the best free retro cyberpunk fonts you can use:

1. **VT323** (classic CRT terminal feel)  
   https://fonts.google.com/specimen/VT323

2. **Press Start 2P** (arcade/retro 8-bit cyberpunk)  
   https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Press+Start+2P

3. **Orbitron** (futuristic neon signage)  
   https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Orbitron

4. **Audiowide** (bold retro synthwave)  
   https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Audiowide

**Quick copy-paste for theme-sync.ts (pre-world + in-world):**

```ts
const cyberpunkFont = `
  body, #main-menu, .setup, .login-form, button, input, h1 {
    font-family: "VT323", monospace !important;
    font-size: 18px !important;
    letter-spacing: 1px;
  }
`;

const style = document.createElement("style");
style.id = "cyberpunk-font";
style.textContent = cyberpunkFont;
document.head.appendChild(style);
```

**Recommendation:**  
Use **VT323** as default — it gives the strongest retro CRT cyberpunk feel. Drop the snippet into your existing `Hooks.once("init")` block in `theme-sync.ts`. It will apply to pre-world menus and in-world UI.

Simple, zero-overhead, and matches your sovereign Ghost Protocol theme.
